How To Cook White Rice

This is a basic guide to cook white rice on a stovetop.

  1. Measure rice using a 2:1 ratio of water:rice.
  2. Rinse rice in cool water and place a medium-sized saucepan on the stovetop.
  3. Add appropriate amount of water to the pan and bring to a boil.
  4. Stir in rice and let simmer on medium-low heat.
  5. Cover the pan with a lid and let it steam for about 20 minutes. Do not lift the lid during this time!
  6. Check the rice after 20 minutes to ensure the water has evaporated.
  7. Turn off the heat and place the lid back on the pan for 5 more minutes.
  8. Fluff rice and serve.

Serving Recommendations

Serve the perfectly cooked rice with lean protein sources or to accompany a variety of meals. Extra rice can be stored in an airtight container for up to 4 days in the fridge.

Notes About Rice Types
