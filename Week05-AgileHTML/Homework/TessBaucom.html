Adopt a cat!

Cats can make for amazing and loving companions. Their independent nature allows for easier schedule flexibility around working and traveling. They're also great mice catchers!


Starter items to buy when adopting a cat:

  • ▪ Cat food
  • ▪ Food and water bowls
  • ▪ Cat litter
  • ▪ Litter box
  • ▪ Cat bed
  • ▪ A few cat toys!

Now that you've got the essentials, you'll need to pick out a friend. Cats can have a variety of personalities, so you'll need to pick a buddy who will suite your personality, needs, and lifestyle. Do you want a lap cat? A good mouser? A cat who likes to be left alone? Going to a shelter to meet the cats is a good way to see which ones will fit your needs.

Odie Odie is a Utah resident, owned by the owners of an Airbnb. He's an outdoor cat, but very friendly and likes to be pet. He enjoys soaking up the sun, too! He's the unofficial greeter of the place, but with his friendliness and enthusiasm, they're happy to let him work for free.
