The stages of Metamorphosis

The four stages of the Metamorphosis are egg, larva, pupa, and adult.The butterfly starts as an egg. Once the egg hatches it becomes a larva. Once the caterpillar reaches full size it becomes a pupa. In the last stage the adult butterfly emerges from the pupa with wings and becomes a butterfly.
