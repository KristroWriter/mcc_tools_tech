Link to another reST file

You need to add a directive to the target file.

In the target file, at the beginning add:

.. _some-unique-id:

Then when you want to link to it:

For details, see
:ref:`some-unique-id`
with more text after it.

The link automagically grabs the title.

Themes

As part of your Sphinx configuration, you can select a theme to set your output look and feel.

As with most tools, you grab one that seems similar to what you'd like, and then customize.

Sphinx comes with the following themes: master , alabaster , classic , sphinxdoc , scrolls , agogo , traditional , nature , haiku , pyramid , bizstyle

See https://rtd-sphinx-theme-sample-project.readthedocs.io/en/latest/

Homework

Document a thing in reStructuredText.

Remember to include the following:

  • Different levels of headings
  • Multiple paragraphs
  • Ordered and unordered lists (preferably with some additional paragraphs that are indented properly)
  • An image
  • A table
  • Inline formatting and links
