Please Bring

  1. Your assigned lunch item.

  2. Receipts (or a record of costs) for all ingredients and supplies, as listed below.

  3. We will do a reckoning so that everyone will have spent the same amount.

  4. 128 or more pre-formed centers in each flavor (You’ll need to double/triple/quadruple some recipes to get this amount. Check your recipe!)

  5. Double Boiler: If you have one, please bring it.

  6. Cookie sheets and trays (all that you have, mark each with your name)

  7. Paper candy cups (about 200–300)

  8. Apron (don’t forget this, you WILL get messy--a couple are available for loan)

  9. Large Plastic Containers or tins in which to take home your finished candy

  10. Candy thermometer (if you have one).

  11. With the leftover chocolate, we make bark. If you have any leftover goodies (e.g., nuts, raisins, dried cranberries) to add to the bark, bring them along.
