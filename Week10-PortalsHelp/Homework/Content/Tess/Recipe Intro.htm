Brownies

Brownies can be easily made from a box mix, but sometimes a homemade batch just hits the spot! These gluten-free brownies are delicious and chocolate-y. Paired with a vanilla, coffee, or other ice cream of your choosing, it's hard to beat!

This recipe technically makes 16 servings, although realistically, it'll be more like 8. Who ever stopped at a single serving of a brownie?
