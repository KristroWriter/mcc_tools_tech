Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F.

  2. Spray your baking dish with non-stick spray and line the bottom with parchment paper.

  3. Whisk together the light brown and dark brown sugar, flour, cocoa powder, and salt in your large mixing bowl.

  4. In the small mixing bowl, combine the eggs, oil, water, and vanilla extract.

  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and, using the rubber spatula, mix until combined.

  6. Add in the chocolate chips (or other desired mix-ins) and fold into the batter until combined.

  7. Pour the batter into the greased pan and bake for 40-48 minutes. You can use a toothpick or a knife and insert it into the center of the brownies; if it is clean when removed, the brownies are done.

Note: It is better to have the brownies be slightly under-done versus overdone.
