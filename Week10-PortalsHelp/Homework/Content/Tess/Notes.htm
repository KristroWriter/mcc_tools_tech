Notes

Brownie recipes don't usually call for baking powder or baking soda, but due to the use of gluten-free flour, it helps keep the brownies from getting too flat or overly dense.

The King Arthur All Purpose Gluten Free Flour tends to work well in most baked recipes, including these brownies. Other brands may work well too, but this type is tried-and-true (and delicious!).

You can use Olive Oil in this recipe, though it will be a noticeable flavor. Some people enjoy the pairing of olive oil and chocolate for a very rich flavor. Canola or Vegetable oil results in a traditional brownie flavor.
