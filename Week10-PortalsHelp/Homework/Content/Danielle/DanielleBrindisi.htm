What is MadCap Flare?

MadCap Flare Software is an amazing technical writing software that allows you to publish to the web, Microsoft Word, and Adobe, just to name a few. This allows for a seamless experience for both users and those reading the content of your work.

How Do I Get Started Using MadCap Software?

  1. Create an account at madcapsoftware.com

  2. Download the software to your PC

  3. Begin working on your project!

What Are The Advantages of Using MadCap Flare?

  • Save time and reduce cost

  • Fairly easy to use

  • Easy to import documents from a variety of other applications
